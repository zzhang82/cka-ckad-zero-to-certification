# Week 12 Resources

These are live operational policies. Verify them against your Linux Foundation portal and order terms; do not rely on remembered summaries.

1. [Exam terms of service](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/exam-terms-of-service) — attempt, retake, and eligibility terms.
2. [Scheduling or rescheduling an exam](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/lf-handbook2/scheduling-or-rescheduling-an-exam) — current scheduling, cancellation, and no-show rules.
3. [CKA/CKAD/CKS FAQ](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/faq-cka-ckad-cks) — final environment, results, simulator, and certificate facts.
4. [Important CKA/CKAD instructions](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/tips-cka-and-ckad) — re-read shortly before the exam and complete the required system rehearsal.
5. [Certified Kubernetes Security Specialist](https://www.cncf.io/training/certification/cks/) — official CKS overview. Passing CKA is the certification prerequisite for attempting CKS.

Never publish portal screenshots, eligibility identifiers, exam feedback details beyond permitted categories, or proprietary simulator content.
