# Week 12 — Contingency and CKS Handoff

> Preview: this is a decision week, not extra content added to fill the calendar.

**Goal:** use the remaining voucher window intentionally: eligible retake, schedule recovery, publication hardening, or bounded CKS planning.

**Before starting:** verify portal dates and live policy from [this week's official resources](RESOURCES.md). The portal and order terms override general summaries.

## Decision paths

1. **Both passed:** record a retrospective and create a bounded CKS backlog. CKA is the required certification prerequisite for CKS.
2. **Eligible retake:** close the evidence-based remediation gate, then schedule within the actual eligibility window.
3. **Attempt not yet taken:** schedule only when readiness evidence and the voucher deadline support it.
4. **Repo contribution:** contribute corrected resources or an original lab without exposing private scores or proprietary tasks.

## Gate

- The chosen action fits the live portal deadline and retake entitlement.
- No no-show or late-reschedule risk is left unexamined.
- Private exam details remain outside Git.
- Future CKS scope is recorded but does not displace CKA/CKAD completion.
