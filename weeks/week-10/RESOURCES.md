# Week 10 Resources

## Recheck before every mock

1. [Current CNCF curricula](https://github.com/cncf/curriculum) — map every mock task to the current CKAD objective and weight.
2. [CKA/CKAD/CKS FAQ](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/faq-cka-ckad-cks) — live version, duration, passing score, retake, and simulator rules.
3. [Important CKA/CKAD instructions](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/tips-cka-and-ckad) — exam mechanics.
4. [ExamUI](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/lf-handbook2/exam-user-interface/examui-performance-based-exams) and [allowed resources](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/certification-resources-allowed) — reproduce the real interaction and documentation boundary.
5. [`kubectl` quick reference (v1.35)](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/reference/kubectl/quick-reference/) — study reference. In mocks, navigate from the `kubernetes.io/docs` domain named by the live allowlist; do not assume the versioned subdomain is separately authorized.

## External calibration

- [Killer.sh](https://killer.sh/) — proprietary, entitlement-only. Use one activation diagnostically and one for final rehearsal when your portal includes it; never copy tasks or solutions.
