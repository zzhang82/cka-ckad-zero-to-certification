# Week 2 Resources

## Read before practice

1. [RBAC authorization (v1.35)](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/reference/access-authn-authz/rbac/) — Roles, ClusterRoles, bindings, subjects, and `auth can-i`.
2. [ConfigMaps](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/configuration/configmap/) and [Secrets](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/configuration/secret/) — references, volumes, updates, and safety.
3. [Liveness, readiness, and startup probes](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/configuration/liveness-readiness-startup-probes/) — distinct failure semantics.
4. [Assign Pods to nodes](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/scheduling-eviction/assign-pod-node/) and [taints/tolerations](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/scheduling-eviction/taint-and-toleration/) — scheduling constraints.
5. [Resource management](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/configuration/manage-resources-containers/) — requests, limits, scheduling, and runtime behavior.
6. [Persistent volumes](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/storage/persistent-volumes/) — PV/PVC binding and failure states.
