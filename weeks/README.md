The route is **CKA first, then CKAD**. Weeks 0 and 1 are runnable now. Weeks 2–12 publish their direction and curated resources early, but a week cannot be marked complete until its original labs, broken-state scenarios, grader, and reset path are implemented and validated.

## Weekly contract

Every implemented week must provide:

1. a small required reading list;
2. retrieval questions before terminal work;
3. a guided lab;
4. at least one broken-state drill;
5. a timed independent check;
6. a deterministic grader and scoped reset;
7. private evidence and a `PASS`, `CONDITIONAL`, or `REPEAT` decision.

Resource collection is capped so it cannot replace practice: one paid-course mapping, a curated official-document set, and no more than two optional community supplements per week.
