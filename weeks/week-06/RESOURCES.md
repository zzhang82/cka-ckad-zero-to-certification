# Week 6 Resources

## Recheck before every mock

1. [CKA/CKAD/CKS FAQ](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/faq-cka-ckad-cks) — live version, duration, score, result, retake, and simulator policy.
2. [Important CKA/CKAD instructions](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/tips-cka-and-ckad) — task-host and terminal behavior.
3. [ExamUI](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/lf-handbook2/exam-user-interface/examui-performance-based-exams) — reproduce the interaction constraints.
4. [Allowed resources](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/tc-docs/certification/certification-resources-allowed) — enforce this boundary during mocks.
5. [Current CNCF curricula](https://github.com/cncf/curriculum) — audit every mock objective against the current CKA PDF.

## External calibration

- [Killer.sh](https://killer.sh/) — use only if included in your portal entitlement. Do not publish its tasks, screenshots, or solutions; reserve activations until an internal baseline exists.

Use the [v1.35 `kubectl` quick reference](https://v1-35.docs.kubernetes.io/docs/reference/kubectl/quick-reference/) for study. In exam-mode mocks, begin at the `kubernetes.io/docs` domain named by the live allowlist rather than assuming the versioned study subdomain is separately authorized.
